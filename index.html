AL-Ahly's Achievements

1907

Establishment of Al-Ahly Club

The establishment of Al Ahly club, first board meeting to offer the club shares to the members of the general assembly.

1909

official opening

The official opening of Al Ahly club, by that time the premises included tracks for the athletic games, football fields, boxing rings, and Weight lifting halls.

1911

first fottball team

The establishment of Al Ahly’s first-ever football team.

1923

first trophy

Al Ahly’s football team succeeds in winning its first every trophy “ Sultan’s cup” after defeating the English team Dragon.

1924

first Egyptian cup

Al Ahly won the Egyptian cup for the first time after defeating Al Sekka Al Hadid.

1926

Only Egyptian membership

The general assembly of Al Ahly took the decision of not approving any non- Egyptian membership in the club.

1929

royal care

Al Ahly decorates its logo with the royal crown after being under the royal care of King Fouad.

football field

Al Ahly opens its football field under the name of prince Farouk before changing it later to El- Tetsh

1944

first swimming pool

Al Ahly opens its first swimming pool as a new facility for its members.

1948

First Egyptian National League

Al Ahly decided to participate in the Egyptian national league for the first time and since then Al Ahly succeeded to win this league title for 9 consecutive times.

1976

First Participation in African Tournaments

Al Ahly decided to participate in The African club tournaments for the first time as the Egyptian league champions.

1982

First-ever African Champions league

Al Ahly succeeded to win its first-ever African Champions league.

1983

El- Khatib wins African Player of the year

Al Ahly’s legend Mahmoud El- Khatib becomes the first-ever Egyptian player to win the African player of the year award.

1984

First-ever African Cup Winner

Al Ahly succeeded to win its first-ever African cup winners cup.

1988

Winning first Afro-Asian cup

Al Ahly succeeded to win its only Afro- Asian cup.

1993

Nasr City Branch

Al Ahly opens its second branch in Nasr City.

2001

African club of the century

Al Ahly was announced the African club of the century by CAF in a very big celebration in Johannesburg.

Al Ahly hosts Real Madrid C.F

Al Ahly hosted Real Madrid C.F. in the century’s game between The African and the European clubs of the century.

2002

First African Super Cup

Al Ahly succeeded to win its first-ever African super cup.

2004

Al Ahly’s first website

In April 2004 Al Ahly Sporting Club launched its first official website, after signing a contract with (Link.net) company during THE ERA OF Al Ahly’s president Captain Hassan Hmady who appointed this task to Mr. Gamal Gabr to be the first editor- In- Chief for the official website. after launching the website, its success was huge that it made the board decide to invest in it by creating Al Ahly media center.

2005

First Participation in FIFA World Cup

Al Ahly participates in the FIFA club world cup for the first time and becomes the first Egyptian team to ever compete on that world-class level.

2006

Third Place in FIFA World Cup

Al Ahly succeeded to win the third place in FIFA club world cup and became the first Egyptian, African and Arabic team to ever win a medal in that international tournament.

2007

100 years Celebrations

Al Ahly celebrated the completion of 100 years with the participation of a group of the elite of the Egyptian community and hosting two of the biggest European teams F.C Barcelona and Benfica to face Al Ahly’s team.

Silver Medal in Handball World Cup

Al Ahly won the silver medal in the handball clubs world cup in an Unprecedented Arabic and African achievement.

handball world championship hosting

Al Ahly hosted the handball world championship hosting.

2012

Foundation stone for Zayed Branch

Al Ahly lays the foundation stone for its third branch in El- Sheikh Zayed.

2014

20th continental trophy

Al Ahly won its 20th continental trophy to become the most titled club in the world with continental trophies over the Italian giants AC Milan.

2019

“2045 vision” Project

Al Ahly Announced its project “2045 vision” which includes Al Ahly Stadium, the development of the Al Ahly’s TV channel, opening a new branch for Al Ahly in Louxor and an Olympic swimming center.

2020

Foundation stone for the fifth settelment branch .

Al Ahly lays the foundation stone for its fourth branch in the fifth settlement.
